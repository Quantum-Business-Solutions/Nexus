Frequently asked

Questions, answered.

What does Nexus do?+
Nexus is a business technology enablement partner. We design, install, secure, and support everything your office runs on — managed IT, cybersecurity, networking, meeting spaces, digital signage, building audio, cellular enhancement, and low-voltage cabling — all from one team.
What areas does Nexus serve?+
We serve growing businesses within about 75 miles of Raleigh-Durham, the heart of the Triangle, and we are expanding across the Carolinas and Virginia.
What size and type of business does Nexus work with?+
We typically partner with multi-office organizations of 20 to 200 employees in professional services, life sciences, commercial real estate, and manufacturing.
How fast is Nexus support?+
We deliver first-call resolution in about 15 minutes with real engineers, backed by 24/7 monitoring and analysis that prevents issues before they arise.
Can Nexus really handle everything from one vendor?+
Yes. One partner, one point of contact, and one point of accountability across your network, security, AV, signage, audio, and cellular — so nothing falls between vendors.
How is Nexus different from a typical MSP?+
A security-first approach, top-shelf support that is never scripted, quarterly business reviews, and a local team that shows up in person. We raise the standard with an enjoyable user experience.
