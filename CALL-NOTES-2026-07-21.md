## "Make it feel less AI"
Shane's read on the first pass was that it felt AI-generated. The fix is specificity, not more polish:
- Write in **their words** (bond/win-win/not-PE/60–80 clients), not generic MSP copy. Done in the Overview + Idea A rationale.
- **Reference the real conversation** (dated 7/21) so it reads as bespoke. Done in both hero eyebrows.
- Ground everything in **their market** (Triangle / Carolinas), not stock personas.
- For the real build: real photography of their team/region, real client quotes, real numbers. Avoid the "five identical themes" feel — present the recommended direction first, others as options.
